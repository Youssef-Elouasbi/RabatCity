Some Places

Hassan Tower

Hassan Tower or Tour Hassan (Arabic: صومعة حسان‎) is the minaret of an incomplete mosque in Rabat, Morocco

Zoo Rabat

Rabat Zoo (Arabic: حَدِيْقَة ٱلْحَيْوَانَات بِٱلرِّبَاط) also known as "Temara Zoo", is a zoological park near Rabat in Morocco that was established in 1973

Rabat Medina

The busy, walled old town of Rabat Medina centers on the Old Market, a souk with alleys of shops selling ceramics, spices, and jewelry
